Career Summary

St. Thomas-born producer, editor, writer and director with more than two decades of experience shaping documentary, branded, nonprofit and narrative content. Experienced in leading production teams, developing story concepts, conducting interviews and delivering polished work under demanding deadlines. Brings a deep Caribbean perspective, global field-production experience and a collaborative, human-centered approach to storytelling.
