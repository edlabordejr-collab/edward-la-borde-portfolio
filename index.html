About Edward

A filmmaker shaped by the Caribbean and driven by meaningful human stories.

Edward La Borde Jr. is a writer, producer, director and editor whose work spans documentary, branded content, nonprofit storytelling and narrative film. His approach combines cinematic craft with emotional clarity, helping audiences connect with people, communities and ideas.

His experience includes global productions, partner impact films, cultural stories and field production throughout the Caribbean and the United States.
